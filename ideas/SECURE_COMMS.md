The communication will be realtime or asynchronous
The communication must be secure in transit and at rest
The communication must be accessible only if both parties agree to it
The communication must be recorded

The communication can be computed against to gain non-indetifying statistics

The technology must all be secured through STIG guidelines and CIS Benchmarks Level II

All technology will be based on Open Source frameworks

The target audience will be clinical psychology practices that want to have a secure, stable, and reliable platform to use for communicating with patients.

AI will be integrated into the system in order to provide patients with insights and information about their sessions, progress, and other such statistics.  This will be optional and only enabled by agreement from the psychologist.
